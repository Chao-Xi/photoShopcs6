1.Creating a layer from a selection
  Pen tool->draw->slection->ok
  Ctrl+J=>create an new layer from selection
  
  Alt+Move tool=>copy everthing in this layer

2.How to turn the background into layer
  double click or right click=>background layer


  Right click new layer=>Flatten images=>(smart object=> background 0);

3.Flatten Images  
  Right click layer panel=>flatten images

4. Combine layers
   1.Resterize layer=>(to pixel)
   2.Merge down(Ctrl E)

5.Linking layers
  Keep multiple layers
  link them together=>move them together

6.Blending Modes
  Layer Panel=>blend model(darken)
  1)Dissolve  2)Darken  3)Regular  4)Mutiply=>best darken 5)color burn  5)Linear burn  6)Darken color


  blend model 2(lighten)
  1)lighten[black part gone white part stay] 2) Screen 3) color dodge 4)linear dodge 

  blend model 3(contrast)
  1)overlay(Use a lot)
  2)soft light
  3)hard light
  4)linear light
  5)hard mix

  blend model 3
  cancellation group (sub divide ..)
  component group (hue, saturation, color, luminosity)

7.Layer Opacity
  layer panel=>opacity
  hotkey:1 2 3 4 5....
